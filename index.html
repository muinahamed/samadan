Daktarbari

Healthcare Information System Ltd. proudly presents “Daktarbhai”, a one-stop digital health service solution for the people of Bangladesh.

Daktarbari

‘Frontliners’ is the first and only networking app for doctors that provides all the service to ease up the life of our frontline warriors, the doctors. ‘Frontliners’ let the respected doctor community of our country to search their suitable job just in a couple of clicks. It also gives our doctors access to personalized news feed based on their individual interest so that our doctors can catch up with the latest treatments and news from the medical community from all over the world easily in their hectic and busy life schedule. To achieve the highest leverage of a digitally connected doctor’s community, Frontliners will keep bringing every necessary services for the doctor and thus be a little footnote to serve the country with a better healthcare service provided by our life saviors, our frontline warriors, the DOCTORS.
